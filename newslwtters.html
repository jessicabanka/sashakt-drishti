Introducing CSD

IGDTUW has taken diverse initiatives setting international benchmarks not only in the academic domain but also in addressing pressing issues of society. The University has made an impact at the individual, family and community levels. The university has launched highly successful social outreach initiatives through the Centre for Sustainable Development aimed at working towards the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Six teams, working under CSD, cover 11 different SDG Goals. All the teams have launched comprehensive campaigns about pressing issues related to the SDGs and have disseminated knowledge to more than a thousand students at large. Our objective to create social leaders that are sensitive towards the preservation of the environment have in turn, sensitized the general public too, and the team’s tremendous accomplishments have brought in results. I’m sure that the endeavours of our students will reach out to the readers of this newsletter and bring about a transformation in the way that they perceive their lives and the lives of others. I wish all the best to the CSD team!

PHASE 1

  • Event 1:Ace Your Preparation with Adira


    Adira took the initiative to prepare students in class XII for their board exams. For the first 60 minutes, students practised for their Term - 1 board exams by taking an MCQ- based Mock test. This test was then followed by a surprise session by honourable Mr Manoj Sethi and Mrs Garima Pant, who addressed the students and answered their questions about their careers as well as board exam strategies.

  • Event 2: Webinar on Entrepreneurship


    ADIRA hosted a session "Let Your Entrepreneurship Dream Come True." The guests for the event - Mr. Daksh Sethi (TedX & Josh Talks speaker, founder - Guby Rogers) and Ms. Manasi Gupta (founder, huesofthemind, public speaker, author) spoke about how to start a brand at the age of 19 and shared their experiences.

ADIRA organized an event: "Chalk Graffiti" at Taarangana '22 to raise awareness. During the event, all interested onlookers were encouraged to express their thoughts using chalk, with the roads as a blank canvas, filled with ideas and thoughts of the students. The initiative of 'Sustainable Sundays' was launched by Cognito wherein posts were released on the social media handles every Sunday to shed some light on the ways to reduce carbon footprints by adopting Green Computing methods. Some of the topics we covered were E-waste, Shutting down computer every night etc.

WHILE WE TEACH, WE LEARN, was an initiative organized by Adira, in collaboration with Girlup Aadya. Mentors and students were required to make a 2-3 minute video explaining a topic from various subjects. These videos would then be uploaded on youtube in the form of playlists for the benefit of students in class 11 and 12.
